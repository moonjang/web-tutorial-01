This course, ARGD 4190: Digital Platforms Fall 2021, is an introduction to screen-based designs and practices in building communicative media for the app and web. We focused on exploring networks as a series of technical tools and codes and as a digital platform, an interactive public space, and a new medium. Through in-class workshops, we studied fundamental UX/UI design and explore the design principles, advanced graphic systems, experimental typography, accessibility for graphic design, and technologies required to build screen-based media.
